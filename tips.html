Emergency Preparedness Tips

Create an Emergency Kit

  • Include non-perishable food items and water for at least three days.
  • Pack essential medications and a basic first aid kit.
  • Include important documents such as identification, insurance, and medical records.
  • Don't forget to include a flashlight, batteries, and a multipurpose tool.

Develop an Evacuation Plan

  • Identify multiple evacuation routes from your home or workplace.
  • Have a designated meeting point for family members or colleagues.
  • Practice evacuation drills regularly, especially with children.
  • Plan for the needs of pets or individuals with special requirements.

Stay Informed During Crises

  • Keep a battery-powered or hand-crank emergency radio for updates.
  • Subscribe to emergency alerts and notifications on your phone.
  • Follow local news sources and official social media accounts for real-time information.
  • Have a list of important contacts and a communication plan for family members.
